Our Story

Built by a Helicopter
Mechanic, for the Industry

After working for many years as a helicopter mechanic on over 15 different types of helicopters in various operational theaters and countries, Jack Barzilay became a supervisor in the Operational Stores of the largest helicopter company in Canada.

It was there where the gap became clear — the industry had no quality swivels to choose from. So Jack started designing and manufacturing his own. Double Dutch Ltd. has now been selling swivels for over 14 years, all over the world.

We listen to our customers and take their suggestions to heart. Incorporating their ideas has resulted in the manufacturing of high-quality, durable swivels that can withstand the harshest environments and helicopter operations.
