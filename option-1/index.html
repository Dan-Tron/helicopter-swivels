// Our History

ENGINEERED
BY EXPERIENCE

After working for many years as a helicopter mechanic on over 15 different types of helicopters in various operational theaters and countries, Jack Barzilay became a supervisor in the Operational Stores of the largest helicopter company in Canada.

It was there where the gap became obvious — the industry had no quality swivels to choose from. So Jack started designing and manufacturing his own. Today, Double Dutch Ltd. has been selling swivels for over 14 years, all over the world, with many satisfied customers in both the helicopter and rigging industries.

We listen to our customers and incorporate their ideas into every product — resulting in high-quality, durable swivels that can withstand the harshest environments and helicopter operations.
